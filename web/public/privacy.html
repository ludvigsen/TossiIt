Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026‑01‑09

This Privacy Policy explains how Organizel (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile app and related services (the “Service”).

Information we collect

  • Account information: when you sign in, we may receive your name, email address, and a unique account identifier from your sign-in provider.
  • Content you provide: text you capture and images you upload/Share to Organizel.
  • App data: basic diagnostics and logs needed to operate the Service (e.g., error logs).

How we use information

  • To provide core features: capture, processing, organizing, and displaying your data.
  • To improve reliability: troubleshoot bugs, prevent abuse, and keep the Service secure.

AI processing

Organizel may process your submitted content to extract structured items (e.g., todos and time-bounded info) and to generate summaries. This processing may involve sending content to an AI provider for analysis.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only as needed to operate the Service (for example, with infrastructure providers) or if required by law.

Data retention

We retain your content for as long as needed to provide the Service. You can request deletion (see below). Some items may be automatically archived or hidden based on the app’s rules.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

  • Access/Deletion: you can request deletion of your account and data (see Delete account).
  • Support: contact us if you have questions (see Support).
