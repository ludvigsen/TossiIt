Delete account

Last updated: 2026‑01‑09

If you want to delete your Organizel account and associated data, use one of the methods below.

Option A — In-app (recommended)

If the app includes an in-app deletion option, use it in Settings.

Option B — Email request

  1. Email support@organizel.com from the email address you used to sign in.
  2. Use subject: Delete my Organizel account
  3. Include: the email address on the account and (optionally) any details needed to identify your account.

What gets deleted

  • Account record
  • Captured notes/images you submitted
  • Derived items (todos, info items, summaries) associated with your account

Timing

We aim to complete deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 30 days).
